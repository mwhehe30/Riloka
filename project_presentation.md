# Riloka - Project Overview Presentation

## Slide 1: Title Slide
**Riloka: Connecting Communities with Local UMKM**

*Platform to discover, support, and grow Indonesia's small businesses*

---

## Slide 2: Problem Statement
**The Challenge**
- Small businesses struggle to gain visibility in digital space
- Consumers find it difficult to discover quality local businesses
- Limited digital presence for UMKM (Usaha Mikro, Kecil, dan Menengah)
- Geographic barriers to business growth
- Lack of centralized platform for local business discovery

---

## Slide 3: Solution
**Riloka: The UMKM Discovery Platform**
- Comprehensive directory of local businesses
- Easy search and filtering for products/services
- Detailed business information and location mapping
- Promotional opportunities for business owners
- Support system for local economic growth
- User-friendly interface for all audiences

---

## Slide 4: Target Audience
**Who Benefits from Riloka?**

**Primary Users:**
- Local consumers seeking products/services
- Tourists exploring local businesses
- Customers looking for authentic local experiences

**Secondary Users:**
- UMKM business owners seeking visibility
- Local government supporting business growth
- Economic development organizations

---

## Slide 5: Key Features Overview
**Core Functionality:**
1. **Homepage Discovery** - Hero section with search and categories
2. **Advanced UMKM Directory** - Search, filter, and sort capabilities
3. **Detailed Business Profiles** - Complete info, photos, maps, products
4. **Category-based Exploration** - 5 main business categories
5. **Promotional Features** - Highlighted deals and offers
6. **Testimonial System** - Customer reviews and feedback

## Slide 9: Technical Architecture
**Modern Tech Stack**
- **Frontend:** Next.js 15.5.6 + React 19.1.0
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS v4
- **Icons:** Lucide React
- **Animations:** Framer Motion
- **Data:** JSON-based API structure
- **Responsive:** Mobile-first design approach
- **Performance:** Optimized loading with skeletons

---
